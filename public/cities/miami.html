Miami

  1. Major Port City – Miami is home to one of the busiest ports in the U.S., contributing significantly to international trade.
  2. International Financial Hub – Miami is known as a major center for finance, banking, and commerce, especially with Latin American countries.
  3. Cultural Diversity – Miami is recognized for its multicultural population, with a large Latin American influence, especially Cuban culture.
  4. Tourism Destination – Known for its beautiful beaches, including South Beach and Miami Beach, as well as vibrant nightlife.
  5. Famous Landmarks – Notable landmarks include the Art Deco Historic District, Little Havana, and the Vizcaya Museum and Gardens.
